Search outside the lines

Your curiosity knows no bounds, so why should your college experience? Study art in Paris, wildlife in the Galápagos Islands or education in Thailand.

LaGrange’s Global Engagement Initiative encourages you to go beyond the walls of the classroom and helps pay for an off-campus travel experience at the end of your sophomore or junior year. Grants of up to $2000 are available to help with expenses.

So pick a destination and join the 68% of LaGrange undergrads who have ‘gone global’—a number that places us among the top 40 baccalaureate colleges* for participation in study abroad.

*According to the 2020 Open Doors Report
